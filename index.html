// Philosophy

The name MOSSKYN joins moss — a quiet symbol of resilience in Native American and Japanese wabi-sabi traditions — with skin. Where life persists through difficulty, beauty becomes inevitable.

Our formulations are built on a bio-identical lipid matrix rich in Vitamins A · D · E · K — the same fat-soluble vitamins skin uses to regenerate. Ancestral wisdom, clinical precision.

// What We Are
  • ›A vertically-integrated skincare house — formula, brand, and channel under one roof.
  • ›A premium D2C engine designed for blended 68–78% gross margins.
  • ›An automated wholesale + affiliate operation already live.
  • ›A flagship-and-franchise growth model paired with a community-rooted CSR program.

Roots Program

Philanthropy that compounds — for skin, for community, for brand.

// The Roots Program

Community sourcing,
community making.

Where MOSSKYN goes, the soil it grew in goes with it.

// What It Is

A workshop series — free, public, hands-on — where local communities help make small batches of MOSSKYN product alongside the brand team. Half craft fair, half ingredient school.

// How It Works

Each event sources a portion of its ingredients locally. Attendees learn formulation basics, take home product, and leave inscribed on the batch label. The community literally goes into the bottle.

// Why It Matters

Brand equity that no ad spend can buy. Customers become collaborators. Communities become brand. And every franchise opens already loved.
