Team Architecture

Every brokerage is 2-4 sided. Each side is a dedicated call team with one job: fill the pipeline on that side.

TEAM A

Supply Side

Asset holders, sellers, producers, service providers with something to offer. Cold outreach to build supply inventory.

Examples: elderly homeowners - SME owners exiting - farmers with surplus - distressed asset holders

TEAM B

Demand Side

Buyers, tenants, enterprises, funds needing what Team A has. Match supply to capital or need.

Examples: PE firms - companies needing space - hospitals with staffing gaps - importers needing finance

TEAM C

Service Referral Layer

Third-party providers attached to every deal. Notaries, lawyers, logistics, care, compliance - referral fees on every transaction.

Examples: notaries - care companies - customs agents - ESG auditors - franchise consultants

TEAM D

Capital Layer (4-sided only)

Institutional buyers who purchase the originated asset/contract at scale. Highest fee per deal. Relationship-driven.

Examples: hedge funds - NPL buyers - green bond investors - royalty streaming cos - hotel PE
